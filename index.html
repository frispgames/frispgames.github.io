The first title in our arsenal of exciting games to come. This spooky circus themed 2D side-scroller will have you challenged and itching to play more. You play as Widdy, the legless skeleton who has been captured and forced to perform in the freak circus at the carnival castle. Your goal is to pilot Widdy through the circus swamp. You'll need to avoid obstacles and projectiles while travelling as far away from the circus as you possibly can. As part of your perilous escape make sure to collect the left over circus tickets scattered throughout to redeem for rewards later at the circus store!
