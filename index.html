The first title in our arsenal of exciting games to come. This spooky and circus themed 2D sidescroller will have you challenged, addictively frustrated and itching to play more. You play as the main character Widdy, the legless skeleton who has been enslaved to perform as part of the freak circus at the carnival castle. Your goal is to pilot Widdy through the circus swamp lands taking care to avoid obstacles and projectiles to escape as far as possible from his withering plight. As part of your perilous escape make sure to collect as many left over circus tickets scattered throughout to redeem for rewards later at the circus store!
